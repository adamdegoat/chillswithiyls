Camping Tip

Always bring more layers than you think you need — even in summer. Temperatures can drop fast once the sun goes down, and being cold at night is the number one reason first-time campers don't go back.

A simple rule: whatever the daytime temperature is, pack for at least 10 degrees colder at night. A cheap fleece or extra blanket takes up almost no space and makes the difference between a great trip and a miserable one.
