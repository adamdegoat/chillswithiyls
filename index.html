You don't need experience. You don't need expensive gear. Camping, overlanding, and off-grid living all start with one simple step — and this is where you take it. Built for everyday people who are curious about a different kind of life.

“The grid is always closer than you think, right up until the moment you leave it.”

Most people who haven't tried off-grid living picture it as some dramatic break from normal life. In practice, the transition is quieter — a few switches turned off, a rhythm adjusted, a dependency quietly dropped. What surprises them is how little they miss and how much more clearly they think.
